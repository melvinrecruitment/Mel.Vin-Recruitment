Why Germany

Career Move

Master's programs at German universities are internationally ranked, often free or low-cost, and open doors to Europe-wide careers.

Eligibility

Are You Eligible?

Check the basic requirements for both pathways below.

Nursing Ausbildung

  • Age 18–35 years
  • Minimum 10+2 (Science preferred)
  • German language B1 or B2 certification
  • Clean health record & background check
  • Willingness to relocate permanently
  • Basic English communication skills
  • Valid Indian passport

Master's Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum 60% aggregate marks
  • English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL) or German B2
  • Motivation letter & statement of purpose
  • Relevant work/research experience (preferred)
  • 2–3 academic recommendation letters
